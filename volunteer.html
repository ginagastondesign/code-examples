Volunteers Often Are the First Point of AMA Contact for Members.

Volunteers help promote the motorcycle lifestyle and protect the future of motorcycling. Volunteers can be involved with AMA-chartered organizations and participate in AMA-sanctioned events regardless of where they live.

Opportunities Are Available for Individual Interests and Skills:

  • Administrative
  • Advocacy
  • Communications
  • Event Operation
  • Fundraising
  • Government Relations
  • Membership
  • Photography
  • Social Media

AMA EAGLES

To prepare members for service, the AMA has developed an on-demand volunteer training program, AMA EAGLES. For more information visit, americanmotorcyclist.com/ama-eagles.

State Chapters

Members who would like to get more involved in advocacy can review the AMA State Chapters Program. For more information visit, americanmotorcyclist.com/ama-state-chapters.

Competition Commissions

Members who would like to participate in racing rulemaking can apply to serve on an AMA Competition Commission. For more information visit, americanmotorcyclist.com/ama-competition-commissions.

Recreational Riding Commissions

Members who would like to discuss ways to enhance or promote recreational riding events can apply to serve on an AMA Recreational Riding Commission. For more information visit, americanmotorcyclist.com/recreational-riding-commissions.
